Kerala Theoretical Physics Summit

Welcome to the official website of the Kerala Theoretical Physics Summit, organized by KTPI in support of research and education. This particular summit is designed to provide a unique platform for students participating in the KTPI Active Research Training (ART) program to present their work. The Kerala Theoretical Physics Summit is dedicated to bringing together researchers and students from various institutions, fostering an environment that encourages the exchange of knowledge and ideas within theoretical physics. Through captivating presentations and thought-provoking discussions, participants will have the invaluable opportunity to share their research findings and forge meaningful connections with like-minded individuals. It enables them to engage with experienced researchers and mentors in their field, enriching their academic journey. Join us at the Kerala Theoretical Physics Summit and join this vibrant gathering that transcends boundaries, sparks intellectual curiosity, and empowers the next generation of theoretical physicists.
