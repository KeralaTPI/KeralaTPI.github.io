Active Research Training (ART) Program


Kerala Theoretical Physics Initiative (KTPI) offers a two-year long active research training (ART) program. ART offers an opportunity for postgraduate-level physics students to work with members of KTPI. A screening process will select a pool of students who will be working on a research project under the mentorship of a KTPI member. In the earlier stages of this program, the students can benefit from a few orientational workshops conducted by KTPI on basic technical and analytical skills. Research areas

ART covers theoretical research in all aspects of physics, including, but not limited to:
  • Statistical physics
  • Gravitation
  • Cosmology
  • Quantum information
  • Condensed matter physics
  • Chemical physics
  • Quantum field theory ...

Who can apply

Highly motivated students pursuing first-year M.Sc. Physics or equivalent at colleges in Kerala can apply for ART.
