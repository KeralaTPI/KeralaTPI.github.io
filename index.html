Kerala Theoretical Physics Initiative

We are a collective of junior faculties, postdoctoral fellows and Ph.D. students across the globe who are originally from Kerala. We have established Kerala Theoretical Physics Initiative (KTPI) to create a home for research and education in the physical sciences by focusing on theoretical approaches.

The idea of KTPI emerged from memorial meetings for Prof. T Padmanabhan (Paddy). It was inspired by the success story of the Trivandrum Science Society. This society was instrumental in nurturing the talents of many prominent physicists like T Padmanabhan, V. P Nair, S. G Rajeev, G Ambika, K Indulekha, etc. KTPI aspires to be such an organization at an all Kerala level.

Presently, our focus is on two main programs: outreach lecture series and mentoring MSc students for research projects. The idea of the outreach lecture series is to bring leading experts in frontier research areas to inform and motivate students.
