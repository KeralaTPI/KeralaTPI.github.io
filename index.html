Kerala Theoretical Physics Initiative

Welcome to the group! Kerala Theoretical Physics Initiative (KTPI) is a collective of faculties, postdoctoral fellows and PhD students across the globe who are originally from Kerala. KTPI aims to create a home for research and education in the physical sciences by focusing on theoretical approaches. Our goal is to establish a research-friendly atmosphere at the undergraduate and postgraduate levels in Kerala. We plan to achieve this goal by fostering strong collaboration among ourselves and with the colleges in Kerala.
